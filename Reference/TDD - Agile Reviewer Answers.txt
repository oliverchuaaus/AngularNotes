Agile Manfiesto:
4 Agile Manifesto items:
Individuals and interactions OVER processes and tools
Working software OVER comprehensive documentation
Customer collaboration OVER contract negotiation
Responding to change OVER following a plan


5 Main Practices:
User stories
Daily Scrum meetings
Fortnight sprints
Peer testing and CI (continuous integration)
TDD (test driven development)

Other Practices:
Kickoff - start a story, and discuss with product owner, BA, tester
Shoulder Check - end of story demo, catch simple defects
Sprint Planning
Backlog refinement or grooming
Retrospect - end of sprint

Gradle no dependencies in Eclipse
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/38171471/eclipse-not-recognizing-gradle-dependencies

Add apply plugin: "eclipse" to build.gradle
Run gradlew cleanEclipse eclipse in cli.
Refresh eclipse
